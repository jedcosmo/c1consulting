As a rapidly growing and entrepreneurial firm, new employees at C1 have the opportunity to build a diverse skill set and take on significant responsibility based on performance. We are a meritocracy where employees progress as fast as their skills allow; we don't have set times to promotion. C1 has a relatively flat structure that emphasizes teamwork and learning from highly experienced, senior team members. All employees are assigned project work and responsibilities in accordance with their capabilities, not their title.
